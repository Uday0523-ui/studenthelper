Career Clarity Test

Answer these 10 questions to get a snapshot of your career readiness.

1. Have you decided which stream you’ll take after 10th/12th?

2. Do you know which career you want to pursue after your studies?

3. Do you know the entrance exams needed for your chosen career?

4. Are you aware of at least 5 careers apart from doctor, engineer, IAS?

5. Do you know what skills are needed for your dream career?

6. Have you spoken to anyone (senior/student/professional) in your chosen field?

7. Do you have a plan for the next 2 years (subjects, exams, etc.)?

8. Do you know your top 3 strengths?

9. Why are you choosing your preferred career?
